## Unbounded Domains
We define 
$$
\int _{c}^\infty f(x) \, dx = \lim_{ r \to \infty } \int _{c}^r \, f(x) dx 
$$
and
$$
\int _{-\infty} ^\infty f(x)\, dx = \int _{-\infty} ^c f(x)\, dx + \int _{c}^\infty f(c) \, dx 
$$
where c is any constant.
